FitNESS stands for Food packaging open courseware for higher education and staff of companies

Get access to free courses

All lectures and interactive contents are provided "AS IS". The content (85 lectures from Common to Specialized Modules) is under development and may contain inconsistencies and inaccuracies. It will be updated regularly.

On the platform, you will find...

Lectures related to multiple food packaging topics including packaging innovations, social impact, food science and more. The content is in free access, no login or email required, is open source and along with the lectures, you have different resources such as guidelines, quizzes, case studies, webinars and more.

The lectures are provided by multiple Universities
